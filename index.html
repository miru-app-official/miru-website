Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Miru handles your information and how your data is used inside the app.

Effective date: [Month Day, Year]

Miru is a habit tracker and reflection app. This Privacy Policy explains how Miru collects, uses, stores and protects information when you use the app or related services.

Miru is designed with a privacy-first approach. Based on the current app setup, most personal habit and reflection data is intended to stay on your device unless future sync or external services are introduced.

1. Information Miru may collect

  • Habit data, including habit names, schedules, completion history, streaks, skips and friction ratings
  • Task data, including tasks, subtasks, projects, due dates and completion status
  • Daily reflection data, including productivity, energy and mood scores
  • Reading data, including books, current progress and reading status
  • Accountability data, including partner codes and locally stored partner connections
  • Subscription status needed to determine access to premium features
  • Basic device or app identifiers used to support app functionality

2. How Miru uses information

  • provide the core app experience
  • save and display your entries, progress and history
  • generate insights, recommendations and pattern analysis
  • support reminders and notifications you choose to enable
  • support subscriptions and premium access
  • maintain app reliability and performance

3. Data storage

Your data may be stored locally on your device. If Miru adds cloud sync, external analytics, crash reporting or other online infrastructure in the future, this Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect those changes.

4. Notifications

If you enable reminders, Miru may schedule local notifications on your device. You can manage notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

5. Subscriptions and payments

If you subscribe to Miru Premium through Apple, payments are processed by Apple through your Apple ID account. Miru does not directly receive or store your full payment card details.

6. Data sharing

Miru does not sell personal data. Data may be shared only in limited circumstances, such as with Apple where needed for app distribution, subscriptions or platform functionality, with service providers if Miru later uses infrastructure like analytics or cloud storage, or where disclosure is required by law.

7. Data retention

Miru keeps your information for as long as needed to provide app functionality, unless you delete it or a longer retention period is required by law.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your personal data.

If you have a privacy-related question or want to request deletion, contact [support@email.com].

9. Children’s privacy

Miru is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

10. Security

Miru takes reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a new effective date.
